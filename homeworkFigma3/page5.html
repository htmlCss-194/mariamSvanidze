Future plans for The Observer template

05.07.2021

First of all, big thank you, as you are reading this, you’ve most likely have downloaded or duplicated the theme from Figma Community.


The Observer is simple and elegant theme which is free to use and we’re going to publich some updates for you to have more flexibility building it as a proper functional website.


Also, we providing design integration to Voog and Wordpress for our templates, read more about the possibilities under About section.


The plans for updating The Observer.

In July

  • Mobile views of the template layouts.
  • CV layout

In August

  • Different menu options (positions, language support etc.)
  • Different header and footer options
  • Different layouts for Blog and Portfolio listing layouts
  • Article page with comments section
  • Gallery option

In September

  • Simple store section (Store listing & product page)
  • Pricelist page

Feel free to contact us if you have questions about the template or need and estimate for integrating the template into functional website in Voog or Wordpress.
